Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my subscription?

If you've purchased the ad-free subscription and reinstalled the app or got a new device, you can restore your purchase for free. Simply go to the subscription page within the app and tap the "Restore Purchases" button.

Is my data collected?

We respect your privacy. The app does not collect any personal identifiable information. All your game data is stored locally on your device. Please review our Privacy Policy for more details.
